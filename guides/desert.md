# Desert Trial

The Desert Trial is a recurring 3-day event dedicated to unlocking and strengthening the hero **Diana**.

## How to Participate
1.  **Collect Clawshards**: Hunt beasts on the map or complete Intel missions.
2.  **Find Dreadwolves**: Use the event menu to locate a Dreadwolf on the map.
3.  **Rally**: Launch a rally, and collect shards.
4.  **Claim**: Claim your rewards for 10 successful rallies.

## Rewards
*   **Diana Shards**: The primary reward. Diana is essential for reducing stamina costs and increasing march speed for PvE.

## Strategy
Set up your auto join in the alliance war menu, so you auto join rallies and collect extra Diana Shards.
