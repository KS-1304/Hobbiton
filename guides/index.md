## Events to Ignore
The following events are generally not worth spending additional resources or speedups on, but you can often still get the Target rewards by just playing the game normally:
* Plan your City
* Power Up
* Develop New Tech
* City Development
* Working Overtime
* Grow your heros
* War Preparations
* Steel Edge
* Armament Competition
* Stand of Arms Event
* Officer Project
