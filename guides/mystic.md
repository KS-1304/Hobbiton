# Mystic Trial

In this minigame, you can test different parts of your skills against NPCs


Coliseum – Heroes, Hero Gear, Hero Exclusive Widget

Forest of Life – Pets, Pet Skills

Crystal Cave – Governor Charm

Knowledge Nexus- Academy Tech, War Academy Tech

Molten Fort – Governor Gear

Radiant Spire – Heroes, Hero Gear, Hero Exclusive Gear, Pets, Pet Skills, Governor Charm, Governor Gear, Academy Tech, War Academy Tech, Skins, Oasis Island bonuses, VIP Levels.

## Best Ratios
- 50/10/40 > Coliseum
- 50/15/35 > Forest of Life
- 60/20/20 > Crystal Cave
- 50/20/30 > Knowledge Nexus
- 60/15/25 > Molten Fort
- 50/15/35 > Radiant Spire
