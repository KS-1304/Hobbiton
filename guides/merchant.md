# Merchant Empire

This event lasts for 7 days, you can dispatch caravans on the first 6 days.

Each day you can Dispatch 4 Caravans.

You should only Dispatch SR or SSR Caravans

It is best to do this during off-peak times.  Avoid immediately after reset.

In addition to Dispatching Caravans you can also raid other players Caravans.

Please avoid raiding Caravans belonging to our Academy.
